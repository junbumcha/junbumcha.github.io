About

I'm research scientist at Kakao Brain, working on various machine learning models towards real-world applications. Specifically, I've worked for few-shot font generation [DM-Font, LF-Font, MX-Font], out-of-distribution generalization [SWAD, MIRO], and open-world segmentation and detection [TCL, PLAC]. I've also worked for various product-oriented projects, including AI Go [Handol], Font generation, OCR [STR], and Healthcare.

My recent research interest is building AGI.
